The Fame Monster

Released 18th November 2009

Producers:

  1. Lady Gaga
  2. Ron Fair
  3. RedOne
  4. Rodney "Darkchild" Jerkins
  5. Fernando Garibay
  6. Teddy Riley
  7. Space Cowboy

The Fame Monster is a reissue of American singer Lady Gaga's debut studio album, The Fame (2008), and was released on November 18, 2009, through Interscope Records. Initially planned solely as a deluxe edition reissue of The Fame, Interscope later decided to release the eight new songs as a standalone EP in some territories.

Side One

  1. Just Dance
  2. Lovegame
  3. Paparazzi
  4. Poker Face
  5. Eh Eh
  6. Beautiful Dirty Rich

Side Two

  1. Just Dance
  2. Lovegame
  3. Paparazzi
  4. Poker Face
  5. Eh Eh
  6. Beautiful Dirty Rich

The Fame Monster received generally positive reviews from music critics.

An electropop record, The Fame Monster has influences of disco, glam rock, and synth-pop music of the 1970s and 1980s, as well as industrial and gothic music. The album was also inspired by fashion shows and runways.

Born This Way

Released 23rd May 2011

Producers:

  1. Lady Gaga
  2. DJ White Shadow
  3. RedOne
  4. Jeppe Laursen
  5. Fernando Garibay
  6. Robert John "Mutt" Lange

Born This Way is the second studio album by American singer Lady Gaga, released by Interscope Records on May 23, 2011. It was co-written and co-produced by Gaga with other producers, including Fernando Garibay and RedOne, who had previously worked with her.

Side One

  1. Marry The Night
  2. Born This Way
  3. Government Hooker
  4. Judas
  5. Americano
  6. Hair

Side Two

  1. Scheiße
  2. Bloody Mary
  3. Bad Kids
  4. Highway Unicorn
  5. Heavy Metal Lover
  6. Electric Chapel

'Born This Way' incorporates a broader range of musical genres such as opera, heavy metal, rock, Europop, electro-industrial, disco, and house in addition to featuring a wider variety of instrumentation and musical styles.

It debuted atop in various major music chart worldwide.
