The Fame Monster

Released 18th November 2009

Producers:

  1. Lady Gaga
  2. Ron Fair
  3. RedOne
  4. Rodney "Darkchild" Jerkins
  5. Fernando Garibay
  6. Teddy Riley
  7. Space Cowboy

The Fame Monster is a reissue of American singer Lady Gaga's debut studio album, The Fame (2008), and was released on November 18, 2009, through Interscope Records. Initially planned solely as a deluxe edition reissue of The Fame, Interscope later decided to release the eight new songs as a standalone EP in some territories. The decision was also because Gaga believed the re-release was too expensive and that the albums were conceptually different, describing them as yin and yang.
